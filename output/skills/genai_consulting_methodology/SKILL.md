# GenAI Consulting Methodology Toolkit

A structured methodology for diagnosing enterprise GenAI maturity (L1-L5) and guiding transformation through an eight-stage consulting process.

## When to use

- "Assess our organization's AI maturity level"
- "Create a GenAI transformation roadmap for the enterprise"
- "What stage of AI adoption is this company at and what should they do next?"
- "Design an AI governance framework for a mid-size organization"
- "Help me run a consulting engagement for enterprise AI implementation"

## How to use

### Step 1: Maturity Diagnosis (L1-L5)

Assess the organization against five maturity levels:

| Level | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| L1 | Initial/Ad-hoc | No formal AI strategy; scattered experimentation |
| L2 | Exploring | Pilot projects underway; basic awareness established |
| L3 | Defined | Formal AI strategy exists; governance emerging; repeatable processes |
| L4 | Managed | AI integrated into core workflows; metrics-driven optimization |
| L5 | Optimizing | AI-native operations; continuous innovation; industry leadership |

Evaluate across dimensions:
- **Strategy & Vision**: Leadership commitment, roadmap clarity
- **Data & Infrastructure**: Data pipelines, compute resources, tooling
- **Talent & Culture**: AI literacy, upskilling programs, change management
- **Governance & Ethics**: Policies, risk management, compliance frameworks
- **Use Cases & Value**: Production deployments, ROI measurement, scaling

### Step 2: Eight-Stage Consulting Framework

1. **Discovery & Stakeholder Alignment** — Map current state, identify sponsors, align expectations
2. **Maturity Assessment** — Apply L1-L5 diagnostic across all dimensions; benchmark against industry
3. **Opportunity Identification** — Prioritize use cases by feasibility, impact, and strategic fit
4. **Architecture & Tool Selection** — Design technical stack (LLMs, orchestration, RAG, agents)
5. **Pilot Design & Execution** — Define success criteria, run controlled pilots, gather evidence
6. **Governance & Policy Framework** — Establish AI ethics, data governance, security, and compliance
7. **Scaling & Operationalization** — Move from pilot to production; build MLOps/LLMOps pipelines
8. **Continuous Optimization** — Monitor KPIs, iterate, advance maturity level

### Step 3: Deliverables

For each engagement, produce:
- Maturity scorecard (current level per dimension + gap analysis)
- Transformation roadmap (90-day, 6-month, 12-month horizons)
- Use case prioritization matrix (impact vs. effort)
- Governance policy templates
- Architecture decision records
- ROI projection model

### Step 4: Practical Application

When advising, consider:
- Start where the organization IS, not where they want to be
- Each level advancement typically requires 3-6 months of sustained effort
- Governance must keep pace with capability development
- Quick wins at current level build momentum for next-level investments
- Bilingual considerations for APAC/Taiwan market contexts
